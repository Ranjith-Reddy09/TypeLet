Typing Tips

  • Keep your fingers on the home row.
  • Start slow and focus on accuracy.
  • Do not look at the keyboard while typing.
  • Maintain a relaxed, upright posture.
  • Practice a little, but regularly.
  • Correct mistakes immediately as you notice them.
